The Problem

The Assisted Living
Workforce Crisis

High CNA and LPN Turnover

Persistent staffing instability disrupts care continuity, increases recruitment costs, and burdens remaining team members.

Manual CE Tracking and Onboarding

Time-consuming manual processes for continuing education tracking and new hire onboarding consume hours of administrative time each week.

Heavy Documentation and MAR Burdens

Medication Administration Records and regulatory documentation create significant time demands that pull staff away from direct resident care.

Reduced Time for Direct Resident Care

Administrative overload diverts staff attention from the core mission: delivering quality, attentive care to residents every day.
